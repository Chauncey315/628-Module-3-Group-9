Japanese Restaurant Analysis

Positive Words

Delicious Fresh Garlic Friendly Ice Large

Insights

  • Taste always comes the first.
  • The serving portions are large enough.
  • People prefer garlic flavor.
  • Servers are usually very friendly here!.
  • Since Japanese restaurants offer a lot of sashimi, freshness is all people care about.

Negative Words

Taste Cream Broth Salty Price

Insights

  • Creamy flavor might be too sickly sweet for most people.
  • Taste of broth might be artificial.
  • Before opening a decent Japanese restaurant, don’t let your place be too pricy!

Our Suggestion

If you want to open a Japanese restaurant in town,

  • Keep your sashimi and vegies as FRESH as you can!
  • Reduce the dishes with too creamy.
  • Save your salt!
  • Don’t make everything too PRICY.
