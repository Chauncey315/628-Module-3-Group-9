Vietnamese Restaurant Analysis

Positive Words

Delicious, Fresh, Pork, Banh mi, Pho, Price, Quick

Insights

  • Vietnamese cuisine contains a lot of fresh vegetables such as spring roll and pho.
  • People prefer Banh mi and pork in Vietnamese restaurants.
  • If you are in rush, go to a Vietnamese restaurants might be a good choice.
  • Vietnamese restaurants always have reasonable price.

Negative Words

Small, food, soup, tofu, noodles

Insights

  • Food portions are always small.
  • The taste of soup might not be good enough.
  • Tofu and noodles are not good choices.

If you want to open a Vietnamese restaurant in town,

  • Consider offering PHO and Pork Belly Banh Mi.
  • Consider opening a GRAB&Go serveice through phone APP.
  • Try more western way to cook Tofu!
