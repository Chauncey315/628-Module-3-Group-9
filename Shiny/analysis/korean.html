Korean Restaurant Analysis

Positive Words

Delicious Dishes Tofu Chicken Worth Spicy

Insights

  • Taste always comes the first!
  • If you want to compete with other Korean restaurants, pay more attention to the side dishes!
  • Korean restaurants always have reasonable price!
  • People prefer spicy food!

Negative Words

Taste Service Soup Cold Busy

Insights

  • Taste always comes the first!
  • Service is always important. Whenever you have busy hours, make sure you have enough waitress/waiters.
  • Korean restaurants always offer ice water. Consider to provide hot tea/water as well might be a good start.
  • Most side dishes are pre-prepared, probably think about warming them up before serving.

Our Suggestion

If you want to open a Korean restaurant in town,

  • Consider offering SPICY FRIED CHICKEN and SOONTOFU JIGAE in your restaurant!
  • Have multiple spicy levels to choose.
  • Warm up the side dishes before serving!
